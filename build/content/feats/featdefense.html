Defense
Home » Feats » Defense

Source: Player's Handbook

Prerequisite: Fighting Style Feature

You gain a defense stance. At the start of each of your turns, you may choose to enter or leave a stance.

Defense Stance. You get a +3 bonus to your AC and a -3 penalty to your damage rolls. Any creature you hit with an attack has disadvantage on its next attack against creatures other than you until the start of your next turn.
