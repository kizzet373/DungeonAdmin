Battle Initiate
Home » Feats » Battle Initiate

Source: Koink

You gain the following benefits.

Weapon Proficiency. You gain proficiency with two weapon of your choice.

Mastery Property. Your training with weapons allows you to choose to gain a mastery property from your choice of two kinds of Simple or Martial weapons that you have proficiency with.

Fighting Style. You gain a Fighting Style Feat of your choice.

Battle Maneuver. You gain a Maneuver Option of your choice and one Superiority Dice, which are d8s, and are used for your Maneuvers. You regain your Superiority Dice after you finish a Short or Long Rest.
