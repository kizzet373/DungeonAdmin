Tavern Brawler
Home » Feats » Tavern Brawler

Source: Player's Handbook

You gain the following benefits.

Enhanced Unarmed Strike. When you hit with your Unarmed Strike and deal damage, you can deal an additional 1d8 plus your Strength modifier instead of the normal damage of an Unarmed Strike.

Damage Rerolls. Whenever you roll a damage die for your Unarmed Strike, you can reroll the die if it rolls a 1 or 2, and you must use the new roll.

Improvised Weaponry. You have proficiency with improvised weapons.

Push. When you hit a creature with an Unarmed Strike as part of the Attack action on your turn, you can deal damage to the target and also push it 5 feet away from you. You can use this benefit only once per turn.
