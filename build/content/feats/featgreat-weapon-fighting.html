Great Weapon Fighting
Home » Feats » Great Weapon Fighting

Source: Player's Handbook

Prerequisite: Fighting Style Feature

Choose one of the following benefits:

  • Ruthless.When you hit a prone creature with a two-handed weapon, you roll an additional weapon die..
  • Stormer.When you hit a creature with a two-handed weapon immediately after moving 20 feet, it has disadvantage on its next attack until your next turn..
  • Ravager.Your attacks with a two-handed weapon increase their chance to score a critical hit by 1 (19 and 20, rather than just 20).
