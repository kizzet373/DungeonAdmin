Archery
Home » Feats » Archery

Source: Player's Handbook

Prerequisite: Fighting Style Feature

You choose one of the following benefits:

  • Deadeye.At the start of your turn, you can reduce your speed by an amount of your choice to add 1 to your attack rolls until the start of your next turn for every 10 feet you reduce.
  • Sniper.When take the attack action, you can forego an attack to make your next attack double in range and deal twice the damage until you move.
  • Combat Archer.When you make a ranged attack in your attack action, you can make an additional melee attack for free until the end of your turn.
