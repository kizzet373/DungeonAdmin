Witch Bolt
Home » All Spells » Witch Bolt

Source: Player's Handbook

Level 1 Evocation (Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard)

Casting Time: Action
Range: 60 feet
Components: V, S, M (a twig struck by lightning)
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute

A beam of crackling energy lances toward a creature within range, forming a sustained arc of lightning between you and the target. Make a ranged spell attack against it. On a hit, the target takes 2d8 Lightning damage.

On each of your subsequent turns, you can take a Bonus Action to deal 2d8 Lightning damage to the target automatically, even if the first attack missed.

The spell ends if the target is ever outside the spell’s range or if it has Total Cover from you.

Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot. All damage increases by 1d8 for each spell slot level above 1.
